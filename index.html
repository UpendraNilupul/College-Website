Welcome to VisionEdu University Sri Lanka

At VisionEdu, we are committed to shaping the future of higher education in Sri Lanka by nurturing innovation, critical thinking, and global perspectives. As a premier institution of learning, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs that are designed to meet the needs of a rapidly evolving world.

Courses We offer

At VisionEdu, we offer a variety of career-oriented and academically rigorous courses designed to empower students with practical knowledge and critical skills. Whether you're just starting your academic journey or looking to advance in your field, our programs cater to all levels of study.

Higher Diploma

The Higher Diploma is a practical, career-focused qualification designed to provide students with advanced knowledge and skills in a specific field. It is ideal for those seeking employment or looking to progress to a degree program. Typically completed in 2 years, it offers both theoretical and hands-on learning.

Degree

Our Degree programs offer a comprehensive academic foundation combined with critical thinking, research, and industry-relevant experience. Spanning 3 to 4 years, our undergraduate degrees prepare students for professional careers or postgraduate studies in their chosen disciplines.

Post Graduation

Our Postgraduate programs are designed for graduates and professionals who wish to deepen their knowledge, specialize in a particular field, or advance their careers. These programs focus on research, critical analysis, and advanced subject expertise, preparing students for leadership roles in academia, industry, and beyond.

Our facilities

At VisionEdu, we provide a range of modern facilities to support academic success and student life. From fully equipped lecture halls and advanced laboratories to digital libraries and student lounges, our infrastructure is designed to create an engaging and comfortable learning environment.

World Class Library

Our state-of-the-art library is the academic heart of VisionEdu, offering a vast collection of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources. Equipped with modern study spaces, computer facilities, and high-speed internet, the library provides an ideal environment for learning, research, and collaboration.

Sport Facilities

At VisionEdu, we value the role of sports and recreation in shaping well-rounded individuals. Our campuses are equipped with top-quality sports facilities, including a multi-purpose sports ground, modern gymnasium, and courts for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and more. Students at VisionEdu actively engage in sports activities.

Computer Lab

VisionEdu offers fully equipped computer labs with the latest hardware and software to support students across all disciplines. Our labs provide high-speed internet, access to industry-standard applications, and a comfortable environment for coding, design, simulations, and research.
